NPU Banners

Overview

Northwestern Polytechnic University (NPU) was in need of marketing banners for college recruitment events. I sat down with the Director of Student Academic Services to gather content ideas and project requirements.

These were the final printed version for the banners that are set on tables. These banners can be used alone or be complimented with the bigger banners. The challenge here was to size limitation for the amount of content that was needed to be conveyed. The best approach was to group the texts to one side and find a suitable (free and unlicensed) picture that was less crowded.

Challenges

The following are the drafts for the large standing banners. The challenge for this one was the lack of information to be displayed and the large amount of space. The goal was to make these banners eye-catching from across the room.

Final Product

These are the final version. By sticking to one image and a single primary color, the banners were able to achieve the desired requirements.
