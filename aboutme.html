Oh hi, I'm Mary — a hybrid designer and developer.

I am currently pursuing a Masters in Information Science with a focus on human-computer interaction and UX / UI design at Cornell University. Previously, I've worked as a front-end developer for a few start-ups in the Silicon Valley where I also explored the craft of designing and developing for a smarter user experience and overall product.

Additionally, I am fascinated with virtual reality. As a Research Assistant in the Virtual Embodiment Lab at Cornell, I'm having fun creating avatars and building virtual worlds. Hopefully one day, there will be a way to experience real-time musical concerts and festivals in the comfort of your own bed!

I always enjoy a good design talk over some tea and/or coffee.
