About Me

Hi, I'm Mary! I am a product designer with programming abilities. I deliver projects from initial UX research stages, through UI design iterations, and prototype high-fidelity mockups with front-end development.

Currently, I am pursuing a Masters in Information Science with a focus on human-computer interaction and UX / UI design at Cornell University. I am also exploring the world of VR as a Research Assistant in the Virtual Embodiment Lab. Hopefully one day, there will be a way to experience real-time live concerts and festivals in the comfort of your own bed!

Previously, I worked in the Silicon Valley as a front-end developer where I got invested in designing and developing for a smarter user experience and overall product.
