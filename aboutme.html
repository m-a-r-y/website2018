About me

How I got into human-centered design

UC Irvine | 2011

Graduated with a Bachelor of Arts in International Studies and Sociology. I enjoy learning and understanding why people do the things that they do.

Exploring Careers | 2012 - 2014

Worked in hospital administration and human resources where I interacted mostly with "non-techy" people and realized how poor designs affect and hinder efficient workflow.

Web Development | 2014 - 2017

Coded as a front-end developer in Silicon Valley in CA. During this time, I learned the technical skill sets to execute designs to take it to production.

Design Work | 2016 - Present

Flexed design skills in web, product, and visual design. During this sprint, I am connecting my interests and past experiences and emphasized on human-centered designs first.

Cornell University | 2017 - Present

Made it to a lovely place in Ithaca, NY to get a Masters in Information Science with a focus on Human Computer Interaction & UI Design.
