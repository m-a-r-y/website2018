Event Application: Take Your Pick

Easing the marketing team’s work at trade events and shows.

Survey and data collection made easy.

COMPANY
Achievers
YEAR
2015
ROLE
Front-end developer
TOOLS
HTML
CSS/Less
JavaScript
Angular.js

Every year, Achievers attends and participates in multiple trade show events, and the marketing team would record attendees' information manually.This inefficient process not only took away valuable time to converse with potential clients, but it was also highly error-prone.

Take Your Pick was created as a solution. The app allows attendees to follow easy step-by-step instructions on entering their information and filling out a simple questionnaire at their convenience at any time. The collected data gets transferred to Achievers' CRM system for the sales and marketing team to access. We also used the information to strategically design marketing campaigns to boost traffic to the Achievers' booth at these events.

DEVELOPMENT PROCESS
CHALLENGES AND SOLUTIONS

Since the final screen displays a different message reflecting the attendees' survey answers, the challenge for this project was to design an app that would be scalable and reusable for future trade shows and events under different circumstances. Also, we had to consider different scenarios and use cases, and create screens to capture each event for a good user experience.

FINAL PRODUCT

The final product had over 7+ screens, each screen asking and collecting different data from participating attendees at events. The final screen is customized depending on the participant’s answers on some of the previous screens hence the two different verisons.
